Littleton, CO, USA

Location

10 miles south of Denver, Littleton is located in Arapahoe, Douglas, and Jefferson counties.

About

Climate

Highs around 90°F, lows near 10°F, and an average snowfall of about 10 inches.

History

The 1859 Pike's Peak gold rush brought settlers who decided to make Littleton their home.

Geography

Located on the South Platte River, the city covers 13.87 square miles with 6.4% water, suburbs, and manmade terrain.

Things To Do

  • Town Hall Arts Center
  • Littleton Symphony Orchestra
  • Western Welcome Week
